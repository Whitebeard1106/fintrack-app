All Features

Everything in one place

Built specifically for personal finance in Uzbekistan — in UZS, with CBU rates, in Russian and English.

Quick Add

Type a free-text phrase like "korzinka 45000 groceries" and the app parses the amount, guesses the category, and logs the transaction instantly. Works in Russian and English.

Balance Dynamics Chart

A line chart shows your running balance day by day. Filter by any month or view all time to spot trends — when you were spending most and when you saved.

Spending Calendar

A monthly heatmap shows how much you spent each day — darker cells mean heavier spending. See your habits at a glance without touching a single table row.

Monthly Budgets

Set a spending limit for any category — Food, Taxi, Rent, Health, etc. A progress bar shows how close you are and the AI insights bubble alerts you when a budget is exceeded.

Savings Goals

Create goals like "Vacation — 5 000 000 сум". Add money to a goal at any time and watch the progress bar fill up. Completed goals show a celebration flag.

Category Analytics

Donut charts break down both expenses and income by category for any period. Hover a slice to see the exact amount and percentage of your total spending.

CBU Exchange Rates

Fetches today's official rates from the Central Bank of Uzbekistan (USD, EUR, RUB, KZT, GBP, JPY, CNY). Cached offline, with a built-in currency converter.

Transaction Search & Edit

Filter your history by description or category in real time. Click any row to edit the date, type, category, description, or amount — or delete it entirely.

JSON Export & Import

Export all your transactions and budgets to a JSON file as a backup. Re-import on any device or browser to restore your full history. Your data, your control.

Exchange Rates

Official CBU rates, always up to date

Tap Refresh to fetch today's official rate for USD, EUR, JPY, GBP, CNY, KZT, and RUB directly from the Central Bank of Uzbekistan API.

Rates are cached in your browser so they're available offline after the first load. A day-over-day change indicator shows which currencies moved up or down.

The built-in converter lets you quickly calculate any amount between UZS and any supported currency — no extra app needed.

Privacy & Data

Your data never leaves your device

FinTrack has no server, no database, no cloud sync. Everything lives in your browser's localStorage.

Stored locally, only

All transactions, budgets, goals, and profile data are written to localStorage in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server, ever.

No registration required

The profile you create (name, email, date of birth) is a local personalization only. There is no authentication, no password, and no account to manage.

Export your own backup

Use the Data panel to download a JSON backup of all your transactions and budgets at any time. Re-import it on any device to restore your history instantly.

Works offline

Once opened, FinTrack works without an internet connection. Exchange rates load from the browser cache. Only the CBU rate refresh requires a network request.
